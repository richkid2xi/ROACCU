About Us

Building Financial Futures Together

ROAA Co-operative Credit Union Ltd. (ROAACCU) was established in November 2011 through the initiative of the National Executive Council (NEC) of the Rubber Outgrowers and Agents Association (ROAA). It was formed to primarily take care of the extra credit facilities requested by the Association's members (farmers) and later, its common bond was opened to embrace the community.

verified

CUA Affiliation

In April 2013, ROAACCU was affiliated to the Ghana Co-operatives Credit Unions Association (CUA) Limited and registered with the Department of Co-operatives in November 2013.

emoji_events

Award-Winning Institution

Multiple national awards including Best Financing MFI in Agriculture & Best Microfinance Methodology and Best Higher Outreach in Rural Areas by GHAMFIN.

hub

5 Networked Branches

Head office at Agona Ahanta with branches in Manso Amenfi, Wassa Simpa, Nzema Ayinase (Western Region) and Assin Foso (Central Region).

ROAACCU is currently among the large Credit Unions in Ghana and the largest Credit Union in the Western and Western North Regions with more than GHS 72M total assets.
